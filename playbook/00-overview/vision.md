# Offload Product Vision

> **Offload your thoughts. We'll help you turn them into action.**

## Mission

People collect more information than ever before, yet struggle to turn it into meaningful progress.

Offload exists to reduce cognitive load. Users shouldn't have to remember where they saved something, decide how to organize it, or build plans from scattered information.

They should simply **offload it**.

Offload will understand it, curate it, transform it, and help users apply it when the time is right.

## Vision

Build an AI companion that transforms scattered information into clear actions, personalized learning, and confident decisions.

## Product Pillars

### Offload

- URLs
- Voice
- PDFs
- Images
- Videos
- Text
- Files

One universal input.

### Understand
AI extracts:
- Intent
- Summary
- Ideas
- Entities
- Relationships
- Similarity

### Curate
Daily Review helps users:
- Keep
- Archive
- Discard
- Merge
- Review Later

### Transform

- Study Guides
- Cheat Sheets
- PDFs
- Quizzes

### Apply
Help users accomplish goals:
- Interview prep
